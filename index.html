About Me

I write and produce original pop music for myself and other artists or organizations, with an emphasis on positive and uplifting messages. Based in Provo, Utah but I mostly work remotely with artists.

Notable Projects

  • “Faith” – Writer, Co-Producer, Singer
    • A vibrant and upbeat contemporary Christian song produced by the LDS Church, loved by millions of youth around the globe.
  • “Choose to Stay” (ft. Dakota Lotus & Sophie Pecora) – Writer, Producer, Singer
    • A suicide prevention song I was hired to make for a mental health PSA by non-profit organization Stand4Kind, featuring Disney Channel’s Dakota Lotus & AGT gold buzzer sensation Sophie Pecora.
  • “Evil Rita” (Parody of "Senorita" by Shawn Mendes) by Ethan Fineshriber – Producer
    • A comical alternative rock parody with over 56 million views on YouTube.
  • “So Perfect” by Lucas Metge – Writer, Producer, Mix Engineer
    • Acoustic love ballad by French popstar based in San Francisco.

Producer

You could say “creative director”... I bring the artist's vision to life and allow them to express themselves more fully with my expertise in audio recording, arranging instruments, complex but subtle alternative pop production, and mix engineering.

Songwriter

I write catchy and compelling modern pop melodies and lyrics that captivate audiences and allow connection between the listener and the message. I specialize in positive messages such as Christian and mental health topics.
