Songwriting Best Practices

Writing a great song is both an art and a science. Below are some of the most important practices you should follow:

  1. Start With a Strong Hook
    • The chorus should be memorable
    • Use repetition wisely
    • Keep the message simple and relatable
  2. Tell a Story
    • Use specific details and imagery
    • Include emotional highs and lows
  3. Revise Relentlessly
